The Artificial Neural Network was written in C (source code) and based on the theory outlined in "Machine Learning" by Tom M. Mitchell. The original aim was to correctly classify all 42,000 training samples from the Kaggle "Digit Recogniser" competition. The network consists of 2,362 nodes and achieved a 99% successful classification rate on the Kaggle training set.
